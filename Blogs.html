The Magic of Christmas Markets in Europe

Christmas markets are a beloved tradition across Europe, originating in Germany during the late Middle Ages. They typically run from late November until Christmas Eve. Cities like Nuremberg, Vienna, and Strasbourg are famous for their stunning decorations, unique handicrafts, mulled wine (Glühwein), and festive food. Understanding Lunar New Year

Lunar New Year, often incorrectly called 'Chinese New Year', is celebrated across many Asian countries including Vietnam (**Tết**), Korea (**Seollal**), and Malaysia. While the date is based on the lunar calendar, the traditions are highly diverse, involving family reunions, special foods (like dumplings or rice cakes), and the giving of red envelopes.
